BMW E30

The BMW E30 is a stance legend—known for its boxy shape, rear-wheel drive, and iconic kidney grille. Often praised by enthusiasts for its tuning potential and track feel, the E30 blends German engineering with modern aesthetic upgrades.

This particular E30 features custom air suspension, wide lips, stretched tires, and a subtle ducktail spoiler. The fitment is tight and the ride sits flush for that perfect show-and-shine finish.

Whether you’re into drifting, Euro styling, or static setups, the E30 remains a go-to build with a strong presence in the stance world.
